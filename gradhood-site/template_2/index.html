Gradhood

Uniting the PhD and Master's Community

Gradhood is a communication platform for the PhD and Master community enabling honest conversation and
publication sharing. We believe its extremely beneficial to build relationships with those that have gone through
what we have gone through - grad school.

This is the PhD and Master's "hood" - uniting us in one place no matter what part of the world we reside in. If
you are a PhD or Master, Gradhood gives you access to your community working in the corporate,
government, academic, or entrepreneurship world.

GradHood is in live alpha and access is restricted as we ramp up. Access is currently invite only. Stay tuned.
